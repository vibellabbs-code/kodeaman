## Analysis pipeline

Findings from every scanner pass through the same steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Normalize">
    Each raw scanner result becomes a `NormalizedFinding` — one schema for severity, confidence, location, evidence, and classification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deduplicate">
    Equivalent findings reported by multiple scanners collapse into one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map to OWASP">
    Findings are tagged with their OWASP Top 10 category where determinable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prioritize">
    The prioritizer scores risk and orders the list. Adapters never set priority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach guidance">
    Remediation, safe examples, and references are added — without inventing detection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What stays out of the way

AspidaSec includes history, telemetry, and a local dashboard, but they are secondary to the CLI report. The product never gates the golden path behind an account, a server, or a UI.
